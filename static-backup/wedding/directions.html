To the Church

From North Dakota

  • Take I-94 E to I-494 S
  • Take exit 71 for County Route 31/Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn left onto Pilot Knob (headed South)
  • Turn left onto Deerwood Dr and right into the parking lot

From South Dakota

  • Take I-35 North to I-35E N
  • Take exit 94 for County Rd 30/Diffley Rd
  • Turn right onto Diffley Rd
  • Turn left onto Pilot Knob Rd destination will be on the right

From the Hotel

  • Head west on Northland Dr toward Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn left onto Co Rd 31/Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn left onto Deerwood Dr

To the Hotel

From North Dakota

  • Take I-94 East to I-494 S
  • Take exit 71 for County Route 31/Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn right at Pilot Knob (heading North)
  • Turn right on to Northland Drive

From South Dakota

  • Take I-35 North to I-35E N
  • Take exit 97A-97B for County Road 31/Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn left on Pilot Knob, head North for 2.4 miles
  • Turn right at the 3rd cross street Northland Drive

From The Church

  • Head North on Co Rd 31/Pilot Knob Rd toward Deerwood Dr for 3.7 miles.
  • Turn right onto Northland Dr

To the Reception

From The Hotel

  • Head west on Northland Dr toward Pilot Knob Rd
  • Turn right onto Pilot Knob Rd
  • Take the 1st right onto Mendota Heights Rd
  • Turn left onto Dodd Rd
  • Turn left onto Mendakota Dr

Please note there is a shuttle from the hotel that will bring guests to the reception and back to the hotel at the end of the night.

From the Church

  • Head north on Co Rd 31/Pilot Knob Rd toward Deerwood Dr for 3.8 miles
  • Turn right onto Mendota Heights Rd
  • Turn left onto Dodd Rd
  • Turn left onto Mendakota Dr

Please note the reception begins at 5:00 p.m. and we are not allowed in until that time.
