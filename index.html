Our Story by Kristi

Our first date was at the Happy Gnome in St. Paul’s beautiful Cathedral Hill neighborhood, where I was living at the time. We met online and only had to exchange a few emails before deciding to meet. Our first date was postponed. I assumed that sneezing in his beer would be a poor first impression, so we rescheduled for the next week, and we’re so glad we did! I meant to arrive at the Gnome early enough to get there first, tell the bartender that I was on a blind date and he should intervene if it looked to be going poorly. When I arrived, Ted was already sitting at the bar in the spot I was planning to sit! I remember seeing him and thinking he was so much cuter than his photos, and I got nervous. After that night, we dated for three years, and had a lot of fun along the way. We love enjoying good food and beer at local places, getting outside, traveling to state and national parks, visiting family and friends, and we spend a lot of time cooking and grilling at home.

Ceremony St. John Neumann

The Ceremony will be held at 2:00 p.m. in the Emmaus Chapel at St. John Neumann Catholic Church in Eagan, Minnesota. This ceremony will be an intimate celebration of the sacrament of marriage in a beautiful space with our dearest friends and family. Please be present with us during the sacrament and refrain from photography or cell phone use.

Reception Mendakota Country Club

The reception will be held at the Mendakota Country Club in Mendota Heights, Minnesota. Cocktail hour will begin at 5:30 p.m. and dinner will be served at 6:30 p.m. You will have some downtime between the ceremony and reception, and we encourage you to visit with other guests back at your hotel or at a nearby pub. We recommend Teresa’s on Highway 110 for their margaritas or the Mendoberri Wine Bar and Café. If you are staying at the Courtyard by Marriott, you may take a shuttle from the hotel to the reception site before the reception, which will allow you to be car-free in the evening should you decide to take advantage of the hotel shuttle to get you back to the hotel safely!
