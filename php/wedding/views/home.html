Welcome to our wedding website. We are excited to celebrate with you!

Ceremony

The Ceremony will be held at 2:00 p.m. at St. John Neumann Catholic Church in Eagan, Minnesota. This ceremony will be an intimate celebration of the sacrament of marriage in a beautiful space with our dearest friends and family. Please be present with us during this sacrament and turn off and put away cameras and cell phones in the church.

Reception

The reception will be held at the Mendakota Country Club in Mendota Heights, Minnesota. Cocktail hour will begin at 5:00 p.m. and dinner will be served at 6:30 p.m. You will have some downtime between the ceremony and reception, and we encourage you to visit with other guests back at your hotel or at a nearby pub as we are not allowed into the reception site early. We recommend the Lone Oak Grill in Eagan for their apps and beer selection or the Mendoberri Wine Bar and Cafe. If you are staying at the Courtyard by Marriott, there will be a shuttle to and from the reception site, so you can leave your car and have a safe ride home!
